Battle Tower Black Screen Glitch

This glitch shows up in the form of a black screen that stays up when you enter the battle room in the Battle Tower in Pokemon Platinum. You still hear beeps if you press the A or B buttons, but the screen remains black. The music still plays, and you may think you need to restart your game but you do not. This glitch ends your current winning streak even though you didn't lose.

When the glitch occurs, you are teleported back to the lobby and the game initiates the same dialogue that happens when you reload the game. The screen remains black, but you can still progress through this dialogue by pressing A or B. Once the dialogue is cleared, you can move around and play normally but the screen will remain black until the graphics are refreshed (ex: opening the party menu or leaving the Tower).

The cause of this glitch is hitting a frame perfect B input during the black screen transition. Do not press the B button at all during this transition to avoid the glitch.

This code responsible is likely leftover debug code, but we don't know for sure. It was remedied in HeartGold and SoulSilver, and doesn't exist in Diamond or Pearl.

Thanks to RETIRE for helping with the investigation.
