Weather Dates

Weather can change on some maps depending on the current date. Most of these changes are cosmetic and do not vary in battle. For example, some days might have heavier rain than others; these look different in the overworld but function the same in battle. Year can be anything for these, all that matters is month and day.

This mechanic has time travel protections, meaning that if you try to modify your system date to go straight to one of these dates, the step decrease won't activate. For Sinnoh, this means the game uses the weather corresponding to January 1st instead. In Johto, this means you are guaranteed hail at Mt. Silver's Peak. Instead, you must change the date to one day before one of these dates, save in game, then wait approximately 24 hours.

Johto

NOTE: Still confirming that there are no other weather changes. In Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver, the only locations that have varying weather are Lake of Rage and Mt. Silver's peak where you battle Red. The Lake of Rage is rainy, but has no weather on Wednesdays. For Mt. Silver you normally experience hail, but on the following dates you experience Diamond Dust:

January 1st January 31st February 1st February 29th
March 15th October 10th December 3rd December 31st

Route 212 Rain
Route 213 None
Route 216 Medium Snow
Acuity Lakefront Medium Snow
Snowpoint City Light Snow
